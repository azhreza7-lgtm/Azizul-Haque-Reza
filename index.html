I am Md. Azizul Haque, a licensed Pharmacist and Sr. Executive based in Barishal, Bangladesh. With a Master of Pharmacy (M. Pharm) and more than 10 years at Opsonin Pharma Limited, I have built deep expertise in pharmaceutical quality control, stability studies, and analytical testing.

My work centres on ensuring that every product leaving the facility meets strict regulatory and safety standards — from long-term stability protocols to analytical report generation and compliance documentation.

I thrive at the intersection of science, precision, and process — translating complex analytical data into clear, actionable insights that support product quality throughout the product lifecycle.
